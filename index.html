About

Support engineer turned UX designer

I spent 6+ years in enterprise SaaS support — at Unqork, Code Climate, and Blink Health — which means I've spent thousands of hours inside the exact moments where products fail users. I know what breaks, what confuses people, and where the UX quietly lets everyone down. That's where my design instincts come from.

I started designing on the side, then freelancing, then going all in. My approach is to find real gaps in existing products — things users are already working around — and design what the fix could look like. I work in Figma, build in HTML/CSS/JS, and I'm currently completing the Google UX Design Certificate.

I'm based in Atlanta, GA and looking for remote UX or product design roles where I can bring both the design thinking and the technical context.
